Chocolate Chip Cookies

Grandma's Rustic Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

My Grandmother would always make the best chocolate chip cookies. She would make the dough by scratch over the course of a week.
This recipe follows her cookies in the old world tradition. It uses freshly ground wheat berries for flour, and takes about a week to make. Please enjoy this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Wheat berries or whole wheat flour
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Baking Powder
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the dough by mxing the dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients together in a separate, smaller mixing bowl.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients together and mix until everything is incorporated.
  4. Add the chocolate chips and mix completely.
  5. Form the dough into small balls and place on a greased baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the oven at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.
